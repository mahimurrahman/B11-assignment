Dr. Muhammad Yunus, the father of microfinance and social business, envisions a world free from poverty and inequality. He believes in creating a global system where every individual, regardless of socioeconomic status, has access to opportunities to improve their lives. His vision emphasizes empowering the underprivileged through innovative financial solutions, such as microcredit, which enables people, especially women, to become self-reliant entrepreneurs. Dr. Yunus advocates for a "social business" model, focusing on addressing social problems rather than maximizing profit.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh's first satellite, Bangabandhu-1, was launched on May 12, 2018, marking a historic achievement. Developed by Thales Alenia Space, it provides telecommunication, broadcasting, and internet services. Named after the Father of the Nation, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, the satellite covers South Asia and parts of Southeast Asia. It enhances connectivity, supports disaster management, and boosts the nation’s digital advancement, symbolizing Bangladesh's progress in space technology.
